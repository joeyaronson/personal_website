ClassPass

Previous Next

ClassPass is a platform for students to assist one another by providing information about a course they have taken. It gives students easy access to helpful tips and logistics about classes they may take in the future. Since the best advice usually comes from peers, we wanted a way to facilitate a way for students to share this advice. This webapp was created using web2py, and Vue.js

E-tendance

Previous Next

With a group of five students I built E-tendance, an Android Application which facilitates in taking attendance for college lectures. It features separate UI’s for both professors and students. E-tendance features two factor authentication to determine whether students were present, by requiring a daily passwords as well as checking the student’s location. Professors can manage their student's attendance history, set lecture location, and send announcements to students. The backend uses Firebase for its database.
